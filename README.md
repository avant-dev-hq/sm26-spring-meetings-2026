### Beyond the Hyperscaler Model

### Key Metrics

### The Distributed Technology Thesis

- **AI Circularity** — Community-controlled data and compute reduces FX exposure, improves resource efficiency across LAC digital ecosystems.
- **Food Security Hedging** — Blockchain food provenance and local processing create inflation buffers for commodity-exposed economies.
- **Community AI** — Distributed model inference reduces dependency on USD-denominated hyperscalers; enables fintech without sovereign data export.

### Structural Headwinds

- **FX Exposure** — Hyperscaler services in USD amplify EM currency vulnerability across digital imports.
- **Skill Drain** — 70% of AI talent concentrated in 5 jurisdictions; LAC tech emigration accelerating.
- **NBFI Fragility** — Volatile $4T nonbank flows; weak institutions equal capital flight acceleration.

## Paper II · Institutional Quality as Capital Infrastructure
### The New Multilateral Imperative

### Key Metrics

| Indicator | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Capital outflow premium (weak institutions) | **0.6pp** | IVA macrofinancial database |
| MENA growth loss | **−2.0%** | IMF GFSR Ch.2 Apr 2026 |
| Tariff impact on growth (ineffective) | **−0.3pp** | IMF WEO Apr 2026 |
| Governance correlation (rule of law ↔ NBFI stability) | **0.67** | IVA 2022–25 |
| Colombia sovereign spread reduction (Apr 2024–Apr 2026) | **−110bp** | Central Bank Colombia |

### Governance as a Macrofinancial Variable

- **Measurable in Basis Points** — 1pp rule-of-law improvement yields −60bp in capital flight premium.
- **Mexico Case** — Governance consolidation drives 1.6% → 2.2% GDP growth trajectory (IMF WEO Apr 2026).
- **Sovereign-Bank Nexus** — 100bp sovereign spread triggers 60bp NBFI yield premium amplification — a contagion accelerant in weak-institution environments.

### Capital Flow Dynamics

- $4T nonbank portfolio inflows to EM represent 70% volatility concentration in the NBFI sector.
- Governance reform signals generate immediate capital stickiness (−100bp yields).
- Tariffs ineffective: −0.2 to −0.3pp growth impact; macro policy dominates trade barrier effects.

## Policy Recommendations

### From Paper I
- **Distributed infrastructure first** — Community-controlled compute reduces USD dependency and EM currency exposure.
- **AI circularity** as fintech enabler — Sovereign data sovereignty without export dependency.
- **Food blockchain** as inflation buffer — Commodity-exposed LAC economies benefit directly.

### From Paper II
- **Multilateral Coordination** — Joint IMF-WBG governance benchmarks: rule of law, judicial independence, transparency indicators.
- **Capital Architecture** — De-dollarize non-tradable sectors; deploy distributed technology for fintech resilience.
- **Governance-Linked Bonds** — Transparent institutional quality metrics reduce capital flight risk premium sustainably.
- **Reject Tariff-Driven Policy** — Evidence confirms −0.3pp growth drag; governance quality drives +2.2% upside (Mexico, IMF WEO Apr 2026).
